Controller Box Evolution

From prototype to refined design, here is the journey of our IoT controller boxes.

Version 1

The first prototype, built with available materials. Simple, functional, but not optimized for design.

Version 2

Improved with 3D printing. Shape was still boxy, but more precise and durable compared to version 1.

Version 3

Latest design: smooth curves, modern white finish, and no longer strictly box-shaped — more aesthetic and user-friendly.

Our Electronic Devices

Prototypes and circuits we developed to manage water and electricity in smart housing.

Circuit for 1 Unit

The first prototype circuit designed to handle water and electricity sensors and actuators for a single unit.

Circuit for 4 Units

A more advanced circuit capable of managing water and electricity sensors for up to four different units simultaneously.

Servo-based Automatic Faucet

A dedicated actuator for water control, shaped as an automatic faucet powered by servo motors.
